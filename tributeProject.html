Tribute

This is the greatest and best website in the world: Tribute.

Long time ago, me and my brother here, we was hitchhikin' down a long and lonesome road. Suddenly, there appeared a developer in the middle . . . of the road. And he said, "Write the best website in the world, or I'll fire you both."

Well, we looked at each other and said, "Okay." And we wrote the first thing that came into our heads--just so happened to be, the best site in the world; it was the best site in the world. Look into our code and it's easy to see: one and one make two; two and one make three; it's binary.

Needless to say, the developer was stunned. A whip-crack with his fountain pen, and the ink had run. He asked us, "Be you programmers?"

And we said, "Yay, we are code brothers. Code oooooooooooooon!"

This is not the greatest site in the world--no, this is just a tribute. Couldn't remember the greatest site in the world. No, this is just a tribute. And the peculiar thing is this, my friends, the site we wrote on that fateful night, it didn't actually look anything like this site.

This is just a tribute. You've got to believe it.
